Lasagna

Description

Making lasagna can be time-consuming, but the results are well worth the wait.

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Onion and garlic
  • Tomatoes
  • Sugar
  • Spices
  • Noodles
  • Cheese
  • Egg

Steps

  1. Make sauce
  2. Cook noodles
  3. Make ricotta mixture
  4. Layer lasagna
  5. Cover with foil and bake
  6. Let it rest
