Schnitzel

Description

This wiener schnitzel is an easy German recipe and one of our favorites. Boneless pork chops can also be substituted for veal and taste excellent!

Ingredients

  • Veal
  • Flour
  • Eggs
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Milk
  • Nutmeg
  • Bread crumbs
  • Butter
  • Lemon

Steps

  1. Pound meat
  2. Dip meat in flour
  3. Beat eggs
  4. Dip meat in eggs
  5. Dip meat in bread crumbs
  6. Cook in butter
  7. Garnish with lemon slices
